Who We Are


At our practice we are focused on providing excellent services with the highest levels of customer satisfaction. We will do everything we can to meet (and exceed) your expectations. Besides improving your smile, one of our main priorities is educating our patients on the importance of oral health and its significant impact on your overall wellness.

Our entire team is dedicated to providing you with excellent, personalized care and service to make your visits as comfortable and pleasant as possible. We’re sure you’ll be happy working with our team. Look around our website and if you have any comments or questions, please feel free to contact us. We hope to see you soon!

Services


From routine cleanings and fillings to nightguards and sleep apnea devices our office is equipped to handle all of your dental needs. To help you understand options we have included descriptions of some of our services on this page.

  • Initial Oral Exam
  • Prophylaxis (Cleanings)
  • Periodontal Treatment
  • Oral Cancer Screenings
  • Restorative (Fillings & Bondings)
  • Crowns and Veneers
  • Root Canals
  • Dental Implants
  • Removable Dentures and Partials
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Mouth Guards
  • Sleep Appliances





Initial Oral Exam:
Your initial oral exam includes a Doctor's exam, full mouth x-rays. diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

Prophylaxis (Cleanings):
For optimal oral health, cleanings are routinely done every 3-6 months by our Registered Dental Hygienists, X-rays and periodontal measurements are taken. Our thorough exams are performed using an intraoral camera.

Periodontal Treatement:
We have a periodontical specialist in our office for patients who require a more involved treatment. Our comprehensive periodontal services range from "deep cleanings" all the way to periodontal surgeries.

Oral Cancer Screenings:
Cancer screening technology has been saving lives for years. We recommend and perform yearly screenings to ensure early detection.

Restorative (Fillings and Bonding):
We use the latest resin materials that bond to the teeth to provide strength and optimal color match.

Crowns and Veneers:
Crowns and veneers can be used to correct, improve and protect teeth. We work with state of the art dental laboratories to fabricate the most naturally esthetic crowns and veneers.

Root Canals:
When we determine that a root canal is necessary we sometimes are able to provide treatment. For more complex cases we work closely with an Endodontic Specialist.

Dental Implants:
We consult with an implant specialist who performs the impant placement. Our office performs the final restoration of the crown or prosthesis.

Removable Dentures and Partials:
To replace missing teeth, we can make removable dentures or partials for you.

Teeth Whitening:
Our state of the art, in office bleaching system will make your teeth whiter in less than 1.5 hours. Another options is our take home bleaching system you can take with you for results in less than two weeks. You can also consider a combination of both systems for long term results.

Mouth Guards (Clenching, Grinding and Atheltic Injury Prevention):
If you clench or grind your teeth while you sleep, a night guard may help reduce your symptoms of TMJ. We custom make mouthguards in a new soft material, inside a hard cover. This provides mroe comfort than the trational all hard appliance

Sleep Appliances:
We recommend a sleep appliance for individuals who suffer from snoring. We also fabricate this appliance for individuals who have undergone an ambulatory sleep study and have been diagnosed with obstructive sleep apnea syndrome. We have experienced good results with the appliance as an alternative to suregery or nasal CPAP.

Get to know our doctors


Dr. Hera Blazer

Having been raised by a woman pioneer in the dental field, it seems that dentistry has always been apart of Dr. Blazer's life. Her first college summer job was being a dental assistant in her Mom's office. That was the beginning of a love affair with dentistry that continues to this day. Dr. Blazer graduated from UCLA with a BA in Bacteriology and recieved her DDS from Northwestern University in 1981. Her 32 years of experience is the foundation upon which she continues to build upon - educating herself and implementing those technological advancements in her office. She has surrounded herself with a committed staff who share the same feeling about dentistry. Dr. Blazer has raised three children - learning the fine art of balancing work and family-while contributing to her community. In her free time she enjoys cooking, hiking, camping and yoga. She is a member of the American Dental Association, the Contra Costa County Dental Association and several Women Dental organizations.

Dr. Stacy Burns

Stacy grew up on the East Coast but is happy to call California her home. She graduated with a BA in Psychology from Cornell University and recieved her DMD from the University of Pittsburg School of Dental Medicine, in 2000. After spending 5 years in the Air Force, she settled in the East Bay with her husband and two young children and has been part of the Blazer Dental family for 7 years.











Dr. Sudabeh Moavenian

Dr Moavenian, a Board Certified Periodontist, has been providing advanced periodontal care and dental implant therapy to patients and doctors since 1994. She completed her dental education at in Omaha, Nebraska (1990) following a doctorate degree in dental medicine in 1885. Her interest in Peridontics and dental implantology took her to the Ohio State University where she obtained her certificate in implantology and a masters of science degree. To become distinguished in the field of periodontology and amongst her colleagues, she then completed the board certification process in 1997. She is currently a member of American Academy of Implant Dentisty(AAID) through Loma Linda Maxicourse Study Club membership, (AAP), American Dental Association and California Dental Association. During your consultation appointments, Dr Moavenian provides a comprehensive evaluation of your oral health and will spend the time it takes to make you comfortable with your treatment options.
